About Me

Pritam (PyyLog)

I've been fascinated by transport ever since I was a child. My curiosity first led me to automobiles, then to airplanes. It was then that I realized I wanted to deepen my knowledge of aeronautics, which led me to pursue engineering studies in this field.

I then discovered computer science and development. I immediately grasped the opportunities for the future, in terms of new technologies. That's what prompted me to specialize in systems, and more specifically in mechatronic systems.

As my scolarship progressed, I became increasingly aware of the growing importance of intelligent technologies in modern systems. This led me to take an interest in artificial intelligence and machine learning, which were once little-known but are now ubiquitous in society. Given this enthusiasm for these technologies, I set myself the challenge of learning more about these fields, both by taking related courses during my degree course, and by learning on my own, in my spare time.

My goal is to be able to work in the aerospace or IT industry as a Machine Learning/Deep Learning engineer or software engineer/software development engineer.
